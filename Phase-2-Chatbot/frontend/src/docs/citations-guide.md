# APA 7th Edition Citations Guide

This guide provides instructions for properly citing sources according to the APA 7th edition format, which is required throughout this textbook.

## Journal Articles

Format: Author, A. A., Author, B. B., & Author, C. C. (Year). Title of article. *Title of Periodical*, volume(issue), pages. https://doi.org/xx.xxx/yyyy

Example:
Smith, J. D., & Johnson, M. K. (2023). Advances in humanoid robotics control systems. *Journal of Robotics and AI*, 15(3), 245-267. https://doi.org/10.1234/jrai.2023.15.3.245

## Books

Format: Author, A. A. (Year). *Title of work: Capital letter also for subtitle*. Publisher.

Example:
Brown, L. M. (2022). *Physical AI: Foundations of embodied intelligence*. Academic Press.

## Book Chapters

Format: Author, A. A., & Author, B. B. (Year). Title of chapter. In A. Editor & B. Editor (Eds.), *Title of book* (pp. xx-xx). Publisher.

Example:
Davis, R. T., & Wilson, S. P. (2023). Sensor fusion in humanoid robots. In K. Anderson & M. Lee (Eds.), *Advanced robotics systems* (pp. 89-124). Springer.

## Conference Proceedings

Format: Author, A. A. (Year, Month). Title of paper. In *Proceedings of Conference Name* (pp. xx-xx). Publisher.

Example:
Chen, X., Liu, Y., & Zhang, W. (2023, May). Real-time control algorithms for bipedal locomotion. In *Proceedings of IEEE International Conference on Robotics and Automation* (pp. 1234-1241). IEEE.

## Websites

Format: Author, A. A. (Year, Month Date). Title of webpage. Site Name. URL

Example:
Robotics Institute. (2023, August 15). ROS 2 documentation. ROS.org. https://docs.ros.org/en/humble/

## Technical Reports

Format: Author, A. A. (Year). *Title of report* (Report No. xxx) [Format]. Publisher. URL

Example:
Thompson, K. L. (2023). *Simulation frameworks for humanoid robotics* (Tech. Rep. R-2023-001) [Technical report]. NVIDIA Research. https://nvidianews.nvidia.com/

## In-Text Citations

### Parenthetical Citations
- One author: (Smith, 2023)
- Two authors: (Smith & Jones, 2023)
- Three or more authors: (Smith et al., 2023)

### Narrative Citations
- Smith (2023) stated that...
- According to Smith and Jones (2023)...
- Smith et al. (2023) found that...

## Reference List Format

- Double-space the entire reference list.
- Use a hanging indent for each reference (first line flush left, subsequent lines indented).
- Arrange entries in alphabetical order by the first author's surname.
- Capitalize only the first word of the title and subtitle, and any proper nouns.

## Special Considerations for Robotics/AI Literature

When citing technical papers in robotics and AI, include:
- Conference/journal names exactly as they appear
- Page numbers when available
- DOI numbers when available
- Version information for software and frameworks
- For arXiv preprints: Author, A. A. (Year). Title of paper. *arXiv preprint*. arXiv:xxxx.xxxxx

Example:
Anderson, P. Q., Rodriguez, M., & Kim, H. (2023). Transformer-based vision-language models for robotic manipulation. *arXiv preprint*. arXiv:2305.12345
